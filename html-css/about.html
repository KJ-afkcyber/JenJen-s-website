5 interesting dog breeds

There are multiple dog breeds such as:

  1. The Golden retriever
  2. The Labrador retriever
  3. The Doberman etc

We are only going to be looking at five starting with:

  • The German shephered

The German shephered is an all- purose dog as it can be a playful companion and as well as a guard dog.It is a large, agile, muscular dog of noble character and high intelligence and the reason why they are the likely breed to be used in police forces and security agencies.It is a herding breed known for its courage, loyalty and guarding instincts. These dogs have a powerful physique,alert and expressive eyes and a distinctive coat that comes in different shades of black and brown.They are very trainable and are very energtic. HOW CUTE!

FUN FACT:These dogs are hollywod stars as they have been featured in countless movies and TV shows.

When we come to maintaining these majestic creatures regular exercise is a must due to their energetic nature.Ensure provision of proper diet to your german shephered as their are a large dog breed.Don't forget to groom and train regularly and constant vet check ups cannot miss to be in your calendar.

  • Pomeranian

The Pomeranian is a small dog breed with a fox-like appearance and a fluffy coat that comes in a variety of colours.They are characteristicly spirited and very docile.Its like owning a small fluffy furball.They are energetic and loyal to their owner.

FUN FACT: Pomeranians have a royal history, having been favorites of Queen Victoria and other European royalty.I can see why!

Regular exercise and health check ups are crucial.Ensure you provide high-quality food that is portioned to avoid obesity and a bowl of fresh clean water.Grooming them constantly will make them look splendid!

  • Siberian Husky

The Siberian husky has a wolf-like appearance and a thick double coat.Its eye color is unique as it can range from blue brown or a mix of both!They are also energetic and their howl cannot be mistaken.

FUN FACT: A group of huskies once saved a small town of Alaska by delivering medicine that was 1000 miles away to the town for a deadly disease.Heroes come in all shapes and sizes!

All the basics; food, water and exercise is a must but for a siberian husky ,regular brushing is key for their thick coats.

  • The French bulldog

Also known as the Frenchie,it is a playful, alert, adaptable, and completely irresistible dog. The French Bulldog resembles a Bulldog in miniature, except for the large, erect 'bat ears' that are the breed's trademark feature. The head is large and square, with heavy wrinkles rolled above the extremely short nose.

FUN FACT: Their cute puppy dog eyes can melt even the hardest hearts.

Proper dieting,regular exercise and grooming, nothing different.Although take note that they are prone to breathing problems in warm conditions and humid areas due to their short snout.

  • The Rottweiler

Rottweilers are large, powerful dogs known for their intelligence, loyalty, and protective instincts. They were originally bred as drover dogs to herd livestock and pull carts. Today, they are often used as working dogs, such as police dogs and guard dogs.They have a robust build and a great physique.

FUN FACT:Rottweilers love to have a job.

Regular exercise,proper diet and grooming are a must but also train them and let them socialize with other dogs.
